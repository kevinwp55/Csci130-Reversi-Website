How To Play

Objective:

Win by having more discs on the game board than your opponent!

How to place Discs?

Two players take turn placing discs on the board. When it is the player's
turn, the board will highlight yellow to identify that the player can place a disc.

Notice The Red Circle and Blue Line

The red circle indicates the player's move in this example.

The blue line indicates that all the black discs in between the first
white disc and the new placed disc will turn over to the player's color.

The red box below indicates the new state of the game (black disc in between
the 2 white discs turned over to white).

Be Intelligent!

The longer the game is played, the more squares have discs on them,
and the more potential discs may get turned over to the other player!


If there are no available moves for a player, the player must Pass

Pass meaning skip the turn


The game is over when the board has no empty spaces or if both players Pass. GLHF!
